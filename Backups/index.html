What is this place?

This is my realm, where I post a great deal of my creative work. Fantasy and sci-fi are my main genres, but I also enjoy mysteries. I make a wide variety of media, from music to stories, to artwork.

How much of this content is AI generated?

The short answer: it's about a 70/30 split, mainly with images. I do most of the writing myself. Let me be clear, I do use AI as a tool to streamline the creative process at times. Brainstorming, idea organization, ect. What I do NOT do is just generate and plop in whatever it spits out. AI is a terrible writer, and image generation isn't much better. Now, I do understand why this would be offputting. AI content is, at present, highly controversial, and I respect why people would resent it. There are plenty of bad actors out there who lazy toss any old slop generated by ChatGPT onto social media and call it a day, and the issue of copyright is a whole other can of worms. I try VERY hard to not be one of those people who abuses it to fart out more gobbledy gook the internet needs as much as a second anus.
That being said, as much as I wish I could do it all, I am just one person. My learning speed is limited, I cannot afford to pay someone else, and there are only so many hours in the day. Therefore, I use AI as a tool to help fill in the gaps. I aim to augment, not replace.
Please don't get me wrong, I'm NOT saying I'm unwilling to learn. Contrary, I'm always trying to improve myself and my knowledge of art, writing, coding, software, etc. There is content here that is 100% made by me, no AI used at all, and if I ever do get to a point where I'm able to replace the AI content with my own, I will do so.
