Designation: Colony 2321

Classification: Active Colony

Security Level: 3

Overview

Following its recent annexation by the Empire, Caelora remains under strict occupation. Civilian populations are contained within designated “Cultural Preservation Zones,” while Imperial forces extract and study the planet's unique gravitic crystal formations that power its floating cities.

Planetary Data

Primary Star: 1 (Sol-type)
Moons: 2
Rotation Period: 268 local days
Climate: Temperate to tropical with volatile upper-atmospheric weather systems
Atmosphere: Rich in ionized gases; stable oxygen composition
Topography: Vast forests, sweeping plains, crystalline mountain ranges, and extensive cloud belts hosting floating settlements.
Unique Feature: Constant auroral displays in the sky

Known Inhabitants

Auraelians: A tall, radiant humanoid species possessing faint bioluminescence. Renowned for their mastery of architecture, kinetic sculpture, and harmonic engineering. Their cities—both terrestrial and airborne—were masterpieces of balance between nature and artistry. The conquest fractured their population; many artisans and philosophers were detained or displaced, while a small number collaborate with Imperial scientists studying Caelora’s crystal technology.

Imperial Status

Colony 2321 remains an officially recognized Imperial holding but is categorized as low priority. The Empire provides minimal oversight beyond trade taxation and limited military patrols. Law enforcement is restricted to municipal zones and is chronically underfunded. Despite periodic crackdowns, criminal syndicates maintain de facto rule over much of the planet's surface.

Addendum

Recent intelligence reports indicate the formation of an inter-syndicate council attempting to unify the planet's criminal factions under a single banner. Imperial analysts predict failure within one cycle, citing “terminal corruption” as the system's defining characteristic.


Security Level Key:
1 - Low Risk: Minimal threat, safe for civilian travel.
2 - Moderate Risk: Some hazards present, caution advised.
3 - High Risk: Significant dangers, restricted access.
4 - Extreme Risk: Severe threats, no unauthorized access permitted.
5 - Critical Risk: Maximum threat level, immediate evacuation required.
