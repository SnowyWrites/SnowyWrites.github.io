Designation: "Novaria"

Classification: Destroyed

Security Level: 4

Overview

Novaria was once a thriving and anomalously vibrant world renowned for its abundance of natural life and the unique energy field permeating its biosphere. The planet was inhabited by the Novarians, a humanoid species possessing innate bio-energetic abilities derived directly from Novaria's planetary core. Their civilization existed in balance with their environment, utilizing this energy—known in classified records as Aetheric Resonance—for communication, healing, and manipulation of natural forces. Following first contact, the Flyu Empire declared Novaria's energy control a strategic threat. Within a decade, the planet was eradicated through orbital bombardment and atmospheric destabilization.

Planetary Data

Primary Star: Dual-Star System (Designations: Helion & Tareth)
Moons: 1 Mega-Moon
Rotation Period: 410 local days
Climate: Formerly temperate and stable, supporting diverse ecosystems across multiple continents.
Status: Total planetary disintegration. Remaining fragments exist as a debris field spanning 0.4 AU.
Unique Features: Residual Arcane energy signatures persist within debris fragments, detectable by long-range spectrometry. Proximity exposure has induced anomalous readings in both biological and mechanical systems.

Known Inhabitants

The Novarian species is presumed functionally extinct. Surviving individuals—estimated fewer than twenty—are scattered across unregistered colonies and fringe worlds. These survivors retain partial Arcane abilities, though diminished due to the loss of their world's resonance field. Recorded manifestations include limited telepathy, empathic projection, and elemental manipulation.

Imperial Status

Classified as a Forbidden Zone under Imperial Directive 7.1. No colonization, excavation, or research is authorized without direct clearance from the Imperial Science Directorate. All data concerning Novarian physiology and Arcane technology is restricted to High Command archives.

Addendum

Unauthorized expeditions have reported persistent electromagnetic interference resembling pulse-wave communication from within the debris field. Analysis suggests these signals may be naturally occurring echoes of Novaria's planetary resonance, though some Imperial archivists speculate they are transmissions attempting to reestablish connection with a world that no longer exists.


Security Level Key:
1 - Low Risk: Minimal threat, safe for civilian travel.
2 - Moderate Risk: Some hazards present, caution advised.
3 - High Risk: Significant dangers, restricted access.
4 - Extreme Risk: Severe threats, no unauthorized access permitted.
5 - Critical Risk: Maximum threat level, immediate evacuation required.
