Designation: "Circuira"

Classification: Dead Planet

Security Level: 4

Overview

Circuira was once home to the Circurians, a technologically superior civilization renowned for their integration of biological and synthetic systems. At their height, the Circurians achieved a seamless fusion between organic life and machine intelligence, effectively extending their lifespan and expanding cognitive capacity beyond natural limits. The Flyu Empire deemed their advancements a potential existential threat and initiated a full-scale extermination campaign approximately one thousand standard years ago. The civilization was annihilated; the planet remains largely intact but lifeless.

Planetary Data

Primary Star: 1 (Sol-type)
Moons: 3 (Formerly used as signal relay stations for planetary wireless networks)
Rotation Period: 400 local days
Climate: Electrically unstable, frequent high-voltage storms
Atmosphere: Marginally breathable; significant pollutant and ion residue
Topography: Urban-dominated terrain; most mountain ranges leveled or hollowed for infrastructure expansion. The few surviving natural regions were once under strict ecological preservation. Shallow, highly conductive bodies of water, dangerous to unshielded equipment.
Unique Feature: Vast skyscraper ruins interconnected by underground tunnel networks originally serving as data conduits, sewer systems, and transport lines.

Known Inhabitants

Officially none. Unconfirmed sightings suggest the presence of autonomous drones, rogue AI fragments, or surviving Circurian constructs maintaining limited self-repair cycles. A handful of surviving Circurians are rumored to exist off-world in hiding, though no verified contact has been established in centuries.

Imperial Status

Circuira is classified as an Imperial Quarantine Zone. Unauthorized entry is punishable by termination or indefinite detention. Despite periodic expeditions by Imperial research divisions and private contractors seeking to salvage Circurian technology, no successful reverse-engineering has been achieved. Several expeditions have failed to return, leading to speculation of residual automated defense systems still in operation.

Addendum

Signal analysis from orbit detects intermittent data bursts originating deep below the planetary surface. Content remains undeciphered. Imperial Science Command has restricted further investigation, citing “unacceptable cognitive corruption risks” from exposure to Circurian neural code.


Security Level Key:
1 - Low Risk: Minimal threat, safe for civilian travel.
2 - Moderate Risk: Some hazards present, caution advised.
3 - High Risk: Significant dangers, restricted access.
4 - Extreme Risk: Severe threats, no unauthorized access permitted.
5 - Critical Risk: Maximum threat level, immediate evacuation required.
