Doc

Role: Chief Medical Officer
Age: 35
Species: Flyu

"Heroes patch up the wounds they didn't cause. I'm just cleaning up my own mess."

Doc was once a model Flyu officer: disciplined, brilliant, and utterly devoted to the cause. His work saved soldiers, refined medical protocols, and for a time, gave him purpose. But then came his transfer to Research Station Theta, home of Project Echo, the Empire's black site where human “subjects” were dissected, studied, and broken in the name of progress.

He lasted a year before the walls of loyalty began to crumble. The breaking point came with Sharron, a young human girl whose psychic abilities the researchers pushed to the brink. When her powers lashed out in terror, the order came down to terminate her. Doc tried to stop it. He failed. They killed her anyway. And something inside him died with her. The next day, he executed his commanding officer and the lead researcher, sabotaged the research, stole a transport ship, and vanished into the void. From that day forward, Doc became the only name he'd answer to.

Reed met him at a rest stop on Sector D Epsilon, and the two bonded over a shared contempt for the Empire. A bonded That would eventually lead to the founding of the HLA.

Now, Doc is serving aboard the Shrike as the HLA's foremost medic and field surgeon, a man who can rebuild a body with precision and break an enemy with the same steady hands. He's stoic, often scowling, but his silence hides a relentless need to make things right, one life at a time.

He keeps Sharron's necklace tucked away under his coat, both as penance, and a reminder that somewhere in the wreckage of who he was, some piece of a soul still remains.
