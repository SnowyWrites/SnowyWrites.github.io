Rook

Role: Gunman
Age: unknown, estimated late 20's
Species: Human

"I'm not a weapon anymore. I just… know how to be one if I have to."

The first thing anyone notices about Rook is his size. Broad shoulders, hands that look like they were built to crush stone, and eyes that have seen too much.

When the HLA found him, he was half-dead in a gutter, veins still pumped full of drugs. The detox alone nearly killed him. The nightmares almost broke him. But he made it through the same way he survived everything else, by sheer force of will. He doesn't remember his name before Rook, but that man, whoever he was, died in the pits somewhere, under the fists of other broken souls and the watchful eye of a Flyu crime lord who forced him to kill for the entertainment of others.

Now he's the crew's shield. The one who steps between danger and the rest of them without thinking twice. He fixes weapons and sews torn uniforms, his huge hands surprisingly deft. He drinks his coffee black, watches cheesy holodramas with Geck, and pretends not to laugh when the little bastard starts singing.

And when the fight starts, when the odds go bad and the walls close in, Rook becomes something else. Not the caged beast the Flyu forged, but the force they couldn't break. A man who learned how to be gentle after the world taught him only to kill.
