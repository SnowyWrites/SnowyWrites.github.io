Jade

Role: Arcane Expert, Healer
Age: 19
Species: Novarian

"The stars remember. Even when we forget."

There's a softness to Jade that disarms even the most battle-hardened soul. A gentleness that feels almost alien in a galaxy so frayed by violence. Her skin glows faintly under starlight, violet and warm, as if the cosmos themselves left their mark on her. The emerald crystal in her forehead gleams with the pulse of distant memories, remnants of Novaria, a world swallowed by fire and silence long before her birth.

She tends to a small tree in her quarters, its roots cradled in a container of luminous soil, its leaves whispering faint green light when she speaks to it. Most think it's just a plant. But to Jade, it's the last living thread of a world that once thrived in harmony with the stars.

When she heals, it's not just mending flesh and bone, it's communion. Energy flows from her hands in ribbons of shadow and light, carrying warmth and sorrow in equal measure. And when she fights, that same power turns to smoke and flame, the shadows twisting with her fury, her voice echoing in the dark like a forgotten prayer.

She doesn't speak much about her past. The others know fragments: an attack, a family lost, a seed planted in the ashes. What they don't see is how often she wakes at night, trembling, whispering to her tree for comfort. How her laughter, soft and fleeting, is as much defiance as joy. Jade is the Voidrunner's soul, the one who reminds them what they're fighting for when the galaxy seems too far gone. The youngest among them, but somehow, the oldest at heart. She carries the weight of extinction with grace, and though she often feels like a ghost among the living, her presence binds them together in ways none of them can explain.
