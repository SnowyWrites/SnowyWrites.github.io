Drake

Role: Heavy Hitter
Age: 36
Species: Flyu

"I ain't proud of what I was. But I'm damn proud of who I fight beside now."

On the surface, Drake seems like all brawn and no brains. A mountain of muscle with a grin too wide for his face and a laugh that could rattle the bulkheads. He's the first to throw an arm around your shoulders, the first to give you a dumb nickname, and the first to step between you and danger without a second thought. He looks like the kind of man who's never known fear. But the truth is, he's built from it.

Drake used to wear Imperial armor. One of countless soldiers who followed orders without question. He thought loyalty meant obedience. Thought heroism came from discipline. Until the day his squad turned a rebel hideout to ash, and he found only bodies beneath the rubble. Women. Children. Humans. The Empire called it a victory. Drake called it what it was: murder.

The medals meant nothing after that. The cheers curdled in his gut. For years he tried to drown the guilt in odd jobs and cheap drink, until the night his blaster jammed mid-trigger against his own head. He took that as a sign that Fate wasn't done with him yet. He sold his home and left, drifting wherever the universe took him.

It was in a dingy motel that Drake ran across a young Novarian girl being harrassed by a drunken Enforcer in the hall outside of his room. He stepped in without a second thought, fighting off the Enforcer and his equally buzzed buddies wearing nothing but a towel. He joined the HLA's ranks shortly after. Now, aboard the Voidrunner, he's traded orders for freedom. He's the ship's brawler and big brother rolled into one. He still laughs loud enough to shake the walls, still cracks bad jokes mid-firefight, but when he says “I've got your back,” he means it with the weight of a thousand ghosts behind him.
