Mirage

Role: Intel Officer
Age: Unknown
Species: Changeling

"Very interesting how humans will run headlong into danger, despite full awareness of their own mortality. 'Spirit', we think is their term for it."

No one knows where Mirage came from. When the HLA first found Mirage, dripping, translucent, and silent, hiding away in the Shrike's coolant chamber, half the crew wanted to flush him into space. The other half couldn't look away as the odd blob attempted to clone itself into a copy of Reed.

Changelings are creatures of myth even in the far reaches of the void. Amorphous beings of water and memory, their true forms nothing more than quivering masses of semi-living gel connected by some form of hive mind. Curiosity drives them more than loyalty, and Mirage is no exception. He developed a deep facination for the members of the HLA and decided to tag along with them and eventually help them, if only to see what they might do.

Over time, Mirage found a strange kind of belonging aboard the Shrike as his curiosity led him to care about the odd bunch of humans he'd adopted. He's since become the HLA's silent infiltrator. An Intel Officer who can slip through security systems and palaces alike, leaving only questions behind.
