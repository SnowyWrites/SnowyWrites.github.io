The Shrike

Model: Starchaser XL67
Crew: 5
Captain: Reed

There's nothing elegant about the Shrike. She's an old fighter, built for endurance, not glory. She's scarred and banged up, but for the HLA, she's home.

The Shrike was rescued from the scrap heap on a desperate gamble. Reed and Doc poured what little they had into buying her when she was barely more than a hollow frame and a half functioning nav core. Then Pierce joined the team, and under her hands the rusted old bird came alive again. Hull plates reforged, engines tuned, systems rewired and augmented. Piece by piece, she became a ship worthy of the rebellion that flies her.

At the heart of her systems is Tin Can, an old SIRv droid Pierce rebuilt as a teen and later integrated into the ship's computer after a near-fatal sabotage left his original body broken beyond repair. TC may lack a voice module now, but he's still brought plenty of personality to the ship. But for all his sarcasm, he's fiercely protective of the crew, especially Pierce, who gave him a second (and third) life.
