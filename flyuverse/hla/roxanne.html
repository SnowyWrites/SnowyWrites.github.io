Roxanne O'Riley

Role: Salvage Officer
Age: 26
Species: Human

"If Geck's the explosion, I'm the one that makes sure the fire spreads right."

Roxanne O'Riley, salvage officer, supply runner, and unofficial voice of reason for the O'Riley twins, has made a life out of turning wreckage into opportunity. Where Geck's chaos burns bright and loud, Roxanne's is quieter, more deliberate. She's got the same smirk, the same wild spark in her green eyes, but behind it sits a calculating mind that can strip a derelict ship for parts before you've even figured out how to turn the lights on.

She lives on Sanctuary 23, maintaining the O'Riley homestead with their grandmother, but she's no stay-at-home mechanic. Roxanne sometimes spends weeks off-world on supply runs, skimming through Imperial wrecks and raiding abandoned mining stations. Her knack for hotwiring, bypassing security systems, and navigating ship graveyards makes her one of the HLA's most valuable assets.

And while she'll never say it out loud, she keeps a close eye on her brother from afar. When Geck's name hits a wanted list... again... she's the one who makes sure his ship mysteriously “finds” replacement parts, or that his next job has a clean escape vector. They may bicker like war dogs, but anyone who dares to come between them will find themselves on the wrong end of Roxanne's plasma torch.
