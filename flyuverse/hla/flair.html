Flair

Role: Rebel Captain
Age: 31
Species: Human

"They called me property. Now I own my fate."

Flair was one of humanity's “finest,” bred, polished, and auctioned to the highest bidder. She was taught to smile, kneel, and obey. Her owner, a Flyu noble, took pleasure in reminding her of it. Often violently. When she dared to question him, he took her eye. When she screamed, he laughed.

He didn't laugh long.

The night she snapped, she didn't plan it. There was no rallying cry just the rush of adrenaline andthe thud of the knife she plunged into his chest while his court watched. After that, she did the only thing she could do: run.

For months, she lived like a ghost, scrounging for food, sleeping in drainage tunnels, hunted across half the sector. The humans she met feared her. The Flyu wanted her dead. But when the HLA found her, Reed saw something beneath the rage and terror, something sharp, something unbroken. Something that the Empire couldn't stamp out.

Years later, she stands at the helm of the Voidrunner, her crew's captain and protector. She's not a woman of many words, but behind that cool exterior lies an iron devotion to her crew. To her, they're not soldiers, they're her family. And woe to anyone who threatens them.
