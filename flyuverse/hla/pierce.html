Pierce

Role: Second Officer, Helmsman, Chief Mechanic
Age: 25
Species: Human

"I was a pet once. They promised they'd take care of me forever. Yeah, that lasted until I wasn't cute anymore."

From pet to pilot, Pierce embodies defiance in motion. Born into captivity under a wealthy Flyu household on Colony 4, Pierce was raised to be ornamental. A living accessory to Imperial vanity. She was pampered, preened, and paraded, until the day her usefulness ran out. When adolescence hit and her “owners” grew bored, they cast her aside into Sector D, the Empire's junkyard of the forgotten.

She should have died there. Instead, she learned to build. She scavenged parts, welded metal, and resurrected broken things, including a derelict SIRv droid she dubbed Tin Can, who became her loyal compaion. Through rust and ruin, Pierce taught herself the art of machines and survival, fixing ships and feeding herself on the thrill of making something work when it shouldn't.

By twenty, she was a legend in the underworld circuits, a racer with nerves of steel and a ship that screamed like thunder. Until a corrupt Flyu official, SB42, sabotaged her craft after losing a fortune betting against “a human.” Her ship exploded mid-race and the crash almost killed her. She was pulled from the wreckage by Doc, the defected Flyu surgeon who would become her crewmate aboard the Shrike.

Now she serves as the Shrike's helmsman, mechanic, and mission control, her hands and mind always moving, always tinkering. Her droid's mind lives on within the ship's systems, reborn in circuits and code. Pierce is sharp-tongued and sharper-minded, a steady voice when chaos reigns. She hides her heart behind dry humor and stoic eyes, but her loyalty runs deeper than she'd ever admit. Those she calls “crew” are family, and she'll fight, and fly, through hell to keep them alive.
