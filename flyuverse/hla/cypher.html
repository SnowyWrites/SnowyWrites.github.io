Cypher

Role: Hacker, Technology Specialist
Age: 63
Species: Circuirian

"Information never dies. It just waits for someone patient enough to find it."

Cypher is one of the last of the Circuirians. A species erased from the stars a millennium ago, their cities now decaying shells of their former glory, their memory fading in static whispers across forgotten servers. Cypher carried these ghosts with him whether he wants to or not, and even since the empire destroyed the only home he ever knew, he's made it his mission to ensure the Empire answers for it's crimes.

Cypher's been a rebel since before the rest of the HLA members were even born. Age 63, while old for a human, is still young and spry for a Circuirian, whose lifespans can stretch for up to two centuries thanks to their cybernetic implants. He began his career as a lone wolf hacker after the Empire laid waste to the small sanctuary he'd called home since his birth, eventually earning a reputation that preceeded him. But after a job gone wrong, the Reed and Doc found him half-starved and flickering in an abandoned server hub, running on backup power. It took time, but Doc rebuilt his cybernetics, and brought him back to full functionality. Cypher returned the favor by becoming a co-founder of the HLA.

Now he's the crew's technological phantom. The one who can make an Imperial ship spin out of control with a single well placed line of code. He doesn't brag, he just does it, with the faintest curl of a smile when it works. When he talks, it's soft, deliberate, and always worth listening to.
