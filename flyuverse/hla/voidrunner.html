The Voidrunner

Model: Novarian Shadowbolt
Crew: 4
Captain: Flair

They found her half-buried in an ancient Novarian ruin, long abandoned, systems silent. Most would see only a relic of a long dead civilization, but the HLA saw potential.

It took months of backbreaking work and blind faith to breathe life into her again. Wires were replaced, conduits repaired, and alien interfaces puzzled over. No one truly understood her, not even after she took to the stars again but The Voidrunner understood them.

At her core grows a luminous tree unlike any known in the galaxy, a living heart that beats with ancient Novarian life force. Its roots wind through the ship's systems, feeding her energy like lifeblood through veins. No reactor, no fusion core—only steady, pulsing light. Under Captain Flair's command, the Voidrunner became home to the second HLA crew: a family of fighters, misfits, and survivors. Cypher has bound the ship's neural grid directly to his cybernetics, becoming half her voice and half her will. And Jade, born of Novarian mystery herself, communes with the tree's silent language, reading its moods, coaxing its power.

Where the Shrike is grit and iron, the Voidrunner is grace and shadow. She moves through the stars like a ghost, swift, quiet, and untouchable. Once a relic of a lost civilization, now a weapon of rebellion.
