Reed

Role: Rebel Captain
Age: 32
Species: Human

"Loyalty's a fragile thing. Once you see what it's really worth, you never hand it out lightly again."

Once a weapon of the Flyu Empire, now a thorn in its side, Reed stands as the embodiment of the Human Liberation Army's fractured spirit: forged by the enemy, but unbroken by it. Born and bred as an Enforcer attack human, Reed was molded from childhood into the perfect soldier: disciplined, obedient, and efficient. For years he served the Empire without question, believing in the cause he was told was righteous.

That belief shattered the day his commanding officer left him behind. A cold order to “leave the human” echoing in his ears as his ship burned and fell from orbit.

He should've died in the wreckage of the Midnight Zone. Instead, fate intervened. The people of Nyx, a neutral world hidden among the ashes of the old trade routes, found him and nursed him back to life. There he discovered not only his humanity, but humanity itself: old Earth history, lost art, and the warmth of a people living free. He fell in love with Ophelia Dante, the daughter of a noble house, and for a time, believed he could start over. But the Empire's shadow is long and love was a luxury he could never truly keep.

Now captain of the Shrike, Reed leads his crew with a quiet authority born of scars and survival. He's calm under fire, tactical to the bone, and capable of turning even the Empire's own playbook against them. Beneath that stoic exterior, though, lingers the heart of a man still searching for meaning among the stars — a man who stares into the void not with fear, but with regret and resolve. He laughs rarely, smirks often, and when he speaks, people listen. To his crew, he's not just a captain. He's the anchor that keeps them from drifting too far apart.
